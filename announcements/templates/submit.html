Style techniques

  • Single *asterisks* or _underscores_ cause italicized words
  • Double **asterisks** or __underscores__ cause bold text
  • # hash-prefixed text causes headers (more hashes cause smaller text)
  • - "space, hyphen, space, text"signifies a bulleted list.
  • Also, to use a line break, use double-spacing - you need to hit enter twice, otherwise it is ignored.
